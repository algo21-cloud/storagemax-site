# StorageMax — Subscription Terms

**Last updated:** May 19, 2026

StorageMax Pro is an auto-renewing subscription that unlocks all features
in the StorageMax app. This page explains exactly how billing, renewal,
trials, and cancellations work — required by Apple App Review and
also just the right thing to be clear about.

## Plans

| Plan       | Price       | Trial          | When offered                            |
|------------|-------------|----------------|-----------------------------------------|
| **Weekly** | $9.99/week  | 7 days free    | New users on the paywall                |
| **Yearly** | $19.99/year | None           | Win-Back offer if you cancel the weekly |

Prices are in USD. Apple may convert to your local currency at the
local-tier equivalent.

## How the free trial works

When you start a StorageMax Pro weekly subscription as a new user:

1. **Today:** You get instant access to all Pro features. No charge.
2. **In 5 days:** Apple sends you a reminder that the trial is ending.
3. **In 7 days:** Your subscription begins charging at $9.99/week.

You can cancel anytime during the trial and won't be charged. Trials
are managed by Apple — eligibility is based on your Apple ID.

## Auto-renewal

Subscriptions automatically renew at the end of each billing period
(weekly or yearly, whichever you chose) unless you turn off
auto-renew at least 24 hours before the period ends. The next
period's charge happens within 24 hours of the renewal time.

## How to cancel

1. Open **Settings** on your iPhone.
2. Tap your name at the top → **Subscriptions** (or **Apple Account
   → Subscriptions**).
3. Tap **StorageMax Pro** and tap **Cancel Subscription**.

You'll keep Pro access until the end of the current billing period,
then it stops. You won't be charged again.

You can also cancel from inside StorageMax: **Settings → Subscription →
Manage Subscription** opens the same iOS sheet.

## Refunds

Refunds for accidental purchases are handled by Apple, not StorageMax.
Open the App Store, tap your profile, tap **Purchased**, find StorageMax,
and tap **Report a Problem**. Apple will review your request.

## Win-Back annual offer

If you cancel the weekly subscription, Apple may show you an offer
for the **Yearly** plan at $19.99/year (≈ $0.38/week). This is a
one-time offer per Apple's Win-Back rules. You can accept or ignore
it. If you accept, the yearly plan starts immediately and auto-renews
yearly at the same price until cancelled.

## Family Sharing

StorageMax Pro is **not** shared via Family Sharing. Each Apple ID needs
its own subscription. Sorry — we may add Family Sharing in a future
release.

## Changes to subscription terms

If we change pricing or terms, Apple will require us to notify
existing subscribers and ask for renewed consent before the new
terms apply. Your existing subscription continues unchanged until
that renewed consent.
